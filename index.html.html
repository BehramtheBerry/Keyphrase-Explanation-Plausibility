Study Information

This study is part of a research project on explainable keyphrase ranking using Large Language Models. Your participation is voluntary and takes approximately 10–15 minutes.

You will be shown 4 scientific abstracts. For each abstract, 5 keyphrases have been automatically ranked as important. Your task is to judge why each keyphrase is important — by selecting the one semantic aspect that best explains the ranking decision.

There are no right or wrong answers. We are interested in your genuine judgment.

The Four Semantic Aspects

For each keyphrase, choose exactly one of these four aspects:

A — Similarity
The keyphrase closely matches the core topic or meaning of the document.
B — Salience
The keyphrase captures the main headline-level theme or central subject of the document.
C — Domain-Specificity
The keyphrase reflects expert or technical terminology specific to the document's field.
D — Essential Knowledge
The keyphrase represents foundational knowledge required to understand the document's core topic.
